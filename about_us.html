About Us

MPAC is a website that allows students pursuing Engineering to buy and sell pre-owned student accessories. This website provides provision to buy and sell used books, workshop tools, calculators and similar accessories. Most of the students are unaware of the methods to purchase student accessories that may be usefull to them for a short span. So instead of purchasing a new product at a higher price this website allows the users to purchase the same product with neccessary quality at a more reasonable price. Similarly in the case of students who like to sell their used accessories which may no longer serve much purpose to them. This is website can be used as platform to find suitable buyers.

Buy

For a user to buy a product they can navigate through the buy section according to their needs and purchase a product which they prefer.An email will be send to the buyer reagarding the details of the seller. They can contact and move on with their dealings after checking the product Also there is a cart facility which will be enable the user to bookmark a product that they wish to purchase later

Request

This website contains a request facility which is included in convenience of the user.This facility helps the user to request for a product that is currently unavialable.When that particular product is avialable in sales the product will be displayed in the request section present in the dashboard.

Sell

Mainly the user can sell Textbooks,Workshop Tools and Calculator in the sell section according to their respective categories.They can also sell other accessories in the Extras section of the sell section The products that the seller posted in the sell section will be avialable in the MY PRODUCTS section which is provided in the dashboard.

MPAC MPAC is a website for students of Mar Athanasius college of engineering where they can buy and sell preused student accessories at a reasonable price.
